BA collection

JINK SS’17 - BA collection. First letters of four Lithuanian words “Jautrumas”, “Išmintingumas”, “Nuoširdumas” and “Kūrybiškumas” are put together into collection’s name JINK. These words represent personal values. “Jautrumas” is sensitivity, “Išmintingumas” means wisdom, “Nuoširdumas” is honesty and “Kūrybiškumas” is for creativity. I believe that these four personal characteristics reflect inner beauty and can reveal the best versions of ourselves. To represent these four values in clothes collection I use aesthetic codes. Sensitivity is coded into layering, wisdom is reflected by asymmetry, honesty is marked as imperfect detail in the garment and creativity is coded into a loose silhouette. Garments can be transformed into different looks using magnets that are fixed in these clothes. Thus person who wears the garment is able to express herself by wearing it in different ways.

Cardholder project

Working with Nosakhari London, I was given a task to create a product that would go together with an AW18 collection. I chose to create a cardholder that includes as fewer details as possible. In this case every cardholder construction has a maximum of two components and just a few stitches. The final model has changed from the sketches having just one seam and elastic tape to ensure a practical using.

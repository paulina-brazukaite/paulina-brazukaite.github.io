Foodie - Recipe Search Website

Project Description

About

Foodie is a recipe platform that helps to find recipes based on the specific products and plan meals in advance.

Type of The Project

The final project of Introduction of Web Design (UX/UI) @CodeAcademy. The research part, user and business goals were done with a group of 3 people. To continue the project, the use of solutions and UI were done by me.

Problem

People struggle every day to think of what to eat. It requires time and attempt to find new recipes that would spice up meal routine. Especially for those who have special requirements for their daily food.

Main Goal

To design the platform that would concentrate on personalised recipes based on the products that you have in your fridge or wish to use for cooking.

User Goals

- Easy way to find recipes that answer user needs.

- Being able to make recipes plan for a desired period of time.

Business Goals

- User's final step is to order ingredients for delivery.

- Recipe search based on the specific products or this could be named ‘what’s left in the fridge’.

- Platform has to include ads to make profit.

Task Flow

Task flow goal: to order ingredients for delivery from the recipe page.

User Flow

User flow goal: to order ingredients for delivery from the recipe page.

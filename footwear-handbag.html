Footwear/Handbag project

I have spent 2015 Spring semester at HAMK University of Applied Sciences (Finland) while studying Footwear Design. One of the tasks was to make a pair of shoes designed by a co-worker. After adjusting the design, I made the patterns and did everything else from the scratch to reach this final result. The tote was designed and made in spare time to complement the footwear.
